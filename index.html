About me

I'm passionate about organizing my life and I want to share that clarity with you. What would it look like for you to be living your life to the fullest right now?

I help people find methods and systems that take into account their individual thinking and learning styles, and people have noted my nonjudgemental style. I've assisted people with ADHD, autism, and various physical disabilities in getting the most out of what they can do.

My background is in systems engineering. As part of my degree, I learned quite a bit about management, human centered design, planning, streamlining, and efficiency.

My day jobs have been in software and data. The most common way of organizing work in software development is iterative, that is to say, by planning a small chunk of work, looking back at how it went, then doing the next chunk. In addition, I practiced balancing streamlining for the future vs. getting things done in the moment.

In my personal life, I like meditating, iced chai tea, and finding the dog at a party.


Services

Project Planning

When you have an idea of what you want to accomplish, but not how to get there
  • We'll envision potential narratives for its completion as well as break down barriers to progress.
  • Regular check-ins are the key to success. How often depends on how much time you have to devote between sessions. I've had clients who benefited from in-depth troubleshooting/planning monthly, weekly, or multiple times a week.
  • Together we'll assign homework to complete by the next session.

Accountability Buddy

When you know what you need to do, but can't seem to stay focused on it
  • I'll keep you on task, and optionally take notes or provide feedback.
