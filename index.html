About Me

I'm passionate about organizing my life and want to share that clarity with you. If you're paralyzed by anxiety, what's in your way? If you find yourself wasting your time, why is that? What would it look like for you to be on top of your game right now?

People note my nonjudgemental style. I've assisted people with ADHD, autism, and various physical disabilities.

My bachelor's degree is in Integrated Systems Engineering in the Human Factors track. Read: human-centered design, change management, mathematical optimization. What's relevant to you: a little prep goes a long way; systematic problems require systematic solutions; you have to listen in order to help.

My day jobs have been in software and data. What's relevant to you: experience with iterative planning; experience balancing streamlining for the future vs getting things done in the moment.

In my personal life, I like board games and iced chai tea.
